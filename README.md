## What Is This?

A curated playlist of 595 free, publicly available live streams — TV shows, movies, music videos, news, sports, and more — organized into browsable categories with a clean dark-themed interface.

No apps to install. No accounts to create. No subscriptions. Just click a channel and watch.

---

## How Do I Use This?

Simply open any channel link in your preferred streaming media player. We highly recommend **VLC**, which is absolutely free with no ads and available on all platforms.

| Platform | Download |
|----------|----------|
| 🪟 Windows | [VLC for Windows](https://get.videolan.org/vlc/3.0.23/win32/vlc-3.0.23-win32.exe) |
| 🍎 MacOS | [VLC for Mac](https://get.videolan.org/vlc/3.0.23/macosx/vlc-3.0.23-intel64.dmg) |
| 📱 iOS | [VLC on App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/vlc-media-player/id650377962) |
| 🤖 Android | [VLC on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.videolan.vlc) |

> **Tip:** In VLC, go to **Media → Open Network Stream** (or **Network** tab on mobile), paste the channel URL, and hit Play.

## Features

- **Real-time search** across all 595 channels
- **Category → Subcategory** drill-down navigation
- **Grouped channel list** with sticky headers and counts
- **Resolution badges** (480p, 720p, 1080p, 2160p)
- **Geo-blocked / Not 24/7 warnings** so you know before you click
- **Duplicate streams labeled** by source (Roku, Tubi, Samsung TV+, Plex, etc.)
- **Direct M3U8 links** — click to open in your player
- **Fully responsive** — works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Single HTML file** — no build tools, no dependencies, no frameworks
- **Dark theme** designed for comfortable browsing

## Hosting It Yourself

1. Fork or clone this repo
2. That's it — enable **GitHub Pages** under Settings → Pages → Deploy from `main`

The entire app is a single `index.html` file with all data embedded. No server, no API, no database.
